You begin by deconsctructing a slurry of biomatter in a birthing vat using a series of pulsed laser emissions. The process is violent and slow. Once the moisture level in the vat reaches the proper value you recalibrate the pulse laser and it begins to flash a rapid and painfully bright series of sickly green pulses. The heartbeat moniter starts to beep as the laser pulses slow. On your console a single button appears underneath the vitals moniters. Press Begin to start the combat trial.
